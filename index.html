Davide Allegro

Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Vision & Robotics

Automation Engineer at IAS-Lab (University of Padova). My research focuses on Multi-camera Calibration, 3D Reconstruction, and Active Mapping.

I am motivated by the vision of human-robot collaboration that prioritizes safety, shared goals, and real-world impact.
